What does "Devoir" mean?

Our North Star

Devoir is a mission-driven startup committed to providing innovative solutions to support health, dignity, and convenience in underserved communities such as Camden.

Our name reflects our commitment — "devoir" means duty in French. We see it as our duty to ensure everyone has access to nutritious, convenient meals that fuel their potential and strengthen their communities.
